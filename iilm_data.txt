IILM University is a private university founded in 1993, located at Plot 16-18, Knowledge Park II, Greater Noida, UP - 201306. It has a 26 acre campus. It was granted University status in 2022 under the Uttar Pradesh Private University Act 2022.

IILM University is accredited by UGC and is also recognised by AICTE and AIU. It has 16000+ alumni and offers 40+ programmes across multiple disciplines. The student to faculty ratio is 1:10.

IILM University Greater Noida is located 500 meters away from Knowledge Park II metro station, making it easily accessible by public transport.

IILM University has campuses in Greater Noida, Gurugram, New Delhi (Lodhi Road), Lucknow and Jaipur.

Scholarships for BTech based on Class 12th marks: 90 to 94.99 percent aggregate gets 50% scholarship, 86 to 89.99 percent gets 30% scholarship, 76 to 85.99 percent gets 20% scholarship, 65 to 75.99 percent gets 10% scholarship.

Scholarships for BTech based on JEE Main score: 97 to 100 percentile gets 100% scholarship, 90 percentile gets 50% scholarship, 87 to 89.9 percentile gets 20% scholarship.

Scholarships for BTech based on CUET score: 95 and above percentile gets 40% scholarship, 90 to 94.99 percentile gets 30% scholarship, 80 to 89.99 percentile gets 20% scholarship, 75 to 79.99 percentile gets 10% scholarship.

BTech eligibility at IILM University is 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry and Maths with minimum 50% marks (45% for SC/ST/reserved candidates). Admissions are based on JEE Main or CUET scores. Both online and offline application options are available. Candidates must pass without grace marks.

Lateral entry to BTech 2nd year is available for diploma holders. Students can apply online at iilm.ac.in/apply or visit campus directly.

IILM University offers a Semester Abroad Programme of 3 to 5 months. Students can study at top universities in France, Germany, Austria, Netherlands and Switzerland. International exposure also includes summer schools after first year, student exchange programmes and global study tours.

Girls get an additional 10% scholarship at IILM University. Scholarships are also available for SC/ST, EWS, defence personnel wards (20% scholarship) and sports quota students. Merit cum means scholarships are available for highly talented students from economically weak families.
